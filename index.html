Welcome to Southern Comfort Pensionne
Located at 203 S. Demeterio St., Maasin City, Southern Leyte, Southern Comfort Pensionne offers a relaxing and convenient stay for travelers and guests. Enjoy free parking, Wi-Fi access, hot & cold showers, and air-conditioned rooms, all in a secure and homey environment.

  • 24/7 Front Desk Assistance and Daily Housekeeping
  • CCTV Security, Generator Backup, and Cable TV
  • Drinking Water Station, Smoking Area, and Access to Nearby Convenience Stores

Whether you're staying for business or leisure, Southern Comfort Pensionne ensures a comfortable and worry-free experience in the heart of Maasin City.

Why Choose Southern CP

Southern Comfort Pensionne is a practical and reliable choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience. With essential amenities like free parking, Wi-Fi, air-conditioned rooms, and hot & cold showers, it ensures a smooth and pleasant stay. Its central location in Maasin City, backed by 24/7 security, front desk assistance, and generator power, makes it an ideal option for both short and extended visits.

What Are They Saying About Us

Southern Comfort is a pension house with a prime location right in front of a constructed Gaisano Mall. The toilet and bath facilities are satisfactory, but there's a definite need for improved cleanliness. The low water pressure makes taking a shower inconvenient. The internet connection is average, allowing for basic tasks. One of the highlights is the helpful and accommodating staff. Additionally, the on-site cafeteria is a convenient option for dining. With some attention to cleanliness and water pressure, Southern Comfort could offer an even more enjoyable stay.
